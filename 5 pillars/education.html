The Five Pillars of our Actions : 4. Education

What We offer

Vision

“Raising a child to make a man or a woman” is a local saying. Hence, it is not surprising that education is a cornerstone of the activities of the foundation.

We support local schools, from kindergarten to high schools, and even college students.

Our goal is to promote excellence because today’s students will be tomorrow’s teachers. Generation after generation, we will certainly have future leaders in our local community ranks.

Activities

Regular consultations with stakeholders in the rural education sector. Mapping and needs assessment of rural schools.

Promotion of excellence through school competitions. Technical and financial support for rural school and educational activities.

Scholarships, financial and material support for needy pupils and students. Mobilize resources (human and financial) to support activities, pupils and students. Construction and equipment of digital centers in rural areas.

Expected results: A “free” access to education for all in rural areas.

Impact

More young people in rural areas; access to NICTs for young people in rural areas, better equipped leaders of tomorrow, young people who learn with pleasure and who pass on their knowledge with pride.
