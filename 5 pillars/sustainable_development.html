The Five Pillars of our Actions : 5. Sustainable Development

What We offer

Vision

The Belinga Foundation promotes Sustainable Development, through Agriculture and all economic activities, vertical and horizontal, tied to it.

The potential is enormous, though the initial investment may be considerable. That’s why the idea of sustainable, durable; development is key.

Time and Effort are our key multiplying factors

Activities

Demonstration of income generating activities (livestock, agriculture, farm schools). Ecotourism (a pilot eco-residence in a rural area).

Recreational activities for tourists (fishing and hunting), guided tours. Technical and financial support for school and educational activities in rural areas.

Expected results: Demonstration of sustainability and circular economy concept.

Impact

A better understanding of the circular economy; a reconversion of practical activities into sustainable activities; more collaboration for agricultural transformation and replication of actions and job creation, banking, modernization and digitalization of entrepreneurship.
