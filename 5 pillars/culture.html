The Five Pillars of our Actions : 2. Culture

What We offer

Vision

Our local cultures have the depth and wealth of centuries, millennia of experience and life.

Rediscovering the past, our history, our ancestors, our customs is the bread that feeds the soul, the pride of the future generations.

We want to showcase that culture, to plant the seed for development, and betterment of ourselves.

The Belinga Foundation sponsors the preservation of that culture, by its celebration.

Activities

Sports tournaments
Cultural weeks
Calls for projects (promotion of culture) for technical and financial support (implementation)

Impact

To promote the pride and courage of peoples by celebrating their cultural habits, sharing their history and bringing them together in sustainable, recurring activities.
